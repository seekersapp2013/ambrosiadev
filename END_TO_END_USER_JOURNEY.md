# Ambrosia - End-to-End User Journey

## 1. Sign Up & Authentication

Users begin their Ambrosia journey by signing up with their email address through the authentication form. Upon registration, they're prompted to create a comprehensive profile including a username, display name, bio, profile picture, and most importantly, their health and wellness interests (such as nutrition, mental health, fitness, or chronic disease management). This interest selection is crucial as it powers the platform's content recommendation engine. Once authenticated, users gain access to the full platform including the home feed, reels, booking system, wallet, and messaging features. The authentication system uses Convex Auth with email-based login, ensuring secure access across all sessions.

## 2. Content Discovery & Access (Articles & Reels)

Users can discover content through two primary channels: the home feed for articles and the reels screen for short-form videos. Articles are accessible via direct links (username/article-slug format) or through the feed, where users can browse based on their interests and tags. When accessing gated content, users encounter a paywall displaying the price in USD tokens and the creator's wallet address. To unlock premium content, users must initiate a payment by copying the seller's wallet address, transferring the specified amount from their wallet, and submitting the transaction hash for verification. Once verified, the content becomes permanently accessible. Free and public content is immediately viewable without any payment. Reels follow the same access pattern - public reels are instantly playable in a vertical, Instagram-style interface, while gated reels require payment verification before viewing. Both content types support engagement features including likes, comments, and bookmarks.

## 3. Wallet Management & Funding

To participate in the platform's economy, users must first generate a cryptocurrency wallet through the wallet section. The system creates a Celo blockchain wallet with a unique address, public key, private key, and recovery mnemonic phrase - all securely stored in the user's profile. Users can view their wallet balance showing both CELO and USD token amounts. To fund their wallet, users navigate to the "Fund Wallet" screen where they receive their wallet address and instructions for transferring funds from external sources like cryptocurrency exchanges or other wallets. The wallet enables users to purchase premium content, book paid sessions with experts, and transfer funds to other platform users. All transactions are recorded on the Celo blockchain, providing transparency and immutability. Users can also view their transaction history and manage fund transfers directly within the platform.

## 4. Booking Sessions with Experts

Users can book consultations with health and wellness experts through the booking browser, which displays available providers with their specializations, job titles, pricing, and availability schedules. The system supports two session types: one-on-one private consultations and group events where multiple participants can join. Users can filter providers by specialization, price range, and search terms to find the right expert. When booking, users select their preferred date and time based on the provider's availability calendar, confirm the session details, and complete payment using their wallet. The booking can be automatically confirmed or require manual approval from the provider depending on their settings. Once confirmed, users receive session details including the LiveKit video call link. To become an expert themselves, users must register as a booking subscriber by providing their credentials (job title, specialization), setting their pricing for both one-on-one and group sessions, defining their weekly availability hours, and describing their expertise and what clients can learn. Experts access their provider dashboard to view pending bookings, manage confirmed sessions, create group events, and update their profile and pricing settings.

## 5. Upcoming Features & Platform Evolution

Ambrosia is evolving with several powerful features designed to enhance care coordination and content quality. The Referrals System will enable experts to recommend up to three other specialists to patients after a session, complete with contextual notes explaining the patient's situation and recommended next steps - allowing seamless care transitions as patients book follow-up sessions with referred experts. A sophisticated Recommendation Engine will leverage user interests and engagement patterns to surface personalized content, ensuring users discover articles, reels, and experts aligned with their health goals. To maintain platform integrity, a Content Moderation System will manually verify all experts before they can publish content or accept bookings, ensuring only qualified professionals provide health and wellness services. This verification process will review credentials, specializations, and professional backgrounds, creating a trusted ecosystem where users can confidently engage with vetted experts and consume reliable health information.
